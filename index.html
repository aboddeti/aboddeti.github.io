I am a graduate student at Purdue University. My current research interests broadly includes the study of many-body interactions in nano-photonic environments. Currently I am trying to understand the role played by the nano-photonic environment in enhancing/suppressing resonant many body dipole-dipole interactions and spontaneous emission.

Bio

I graduated from UM-DAE Center for Excellence in Basic Sciences with an Int. M.Sc in Physics.
